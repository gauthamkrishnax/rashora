Engineering Design & Drafting

Rashora Engineering and Management Solutions offers engineering design & technical drawing services that bring your ideas to life. Our team work together to ensure every project is executed with technical excellence and precision. This helps your clients with project planning and execution. Our services include conceptual & detailed engineering design, 2D Drafting & 3D Modeling and Multi-disciplinary drafting services.

We provide technical drawing services for projects of all sizes and our designs meet your functional, aesthetic, and regulatory requirements. The concept development stage involves taking an initial idea or requirement and shaping it into a clear, workable concept. The next stage deals with confirming whether the design is practical, cost-effective, and technically viable. Once the concept is approved, the technical details such as drawings, specifications, and all documentation are worked out in the next stage. Finally, the design is refined to improve performance, reduce material use, cut costs, and ensure compliance.

Our experts use highly advanced CAD software to develop highly accurate 2D and 3D drawings. This ensures good communication and implementation. 2D drafting includes floor plans, elevations, sections and schematics. 3D modelling involves creating a realistic visualisation of how the final product or structure will look.

We offer specialized drafting services across multiple engineering disciplines. Mechanical drafting involves creating architectural technical drawings of mechanical components and systems. Electrical drafting includes drawings related to electrical systems. Civil & structural drafting consists of drawings for civil engineering projects. HVAC and plumbing drafting come under specialized drafting for mechanical systems that ensure comfort and functionality in buildings.
