Electrical System Installation & Services

Looking for a complete electrical installation service for your building, Rashora Engineering and Management Solutions, one of the best electrical contractors in Kerala, is here for you. We offer advanced electrical system solutions customized for industrial, commercial and any infrastructure projects. Our comprehensive services include initial design to final commissioning. We value safety first by offering an efficient and secure electrical installation service.

Our services start with thorough planning and system design, which includes choosing transformers, switchgear, and protective devices as well as load calculations and panel scheduling. We ensure compliance and dependability in every project by closely adhering to national and international safety regulations.

We have a team specialized in installing low tension (LT) electrical systems for residential and commercial buildings and high tension (HT) electrical systems for industrial applications. It includes cable laying, panel erection, conduit works, and transformer setup. Every task, whether it involves a 33kV substation or an internal building distribution system, is carried out with care and precision by our professionals.

Our services don't end with just installation, as we also provide electrical maintenance services. We carry out extensive testing and commissioning, including system energization, relay coordination, insulation resistance, and earth testing. Prior to handover, each installation is carefully documented and certified safe.
