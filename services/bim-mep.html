FAQs

What is BIM?

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is a digital process that creates detailed 3D models of buildings to improve design, planning, and construction efficiency.

What does MEP stand for?

MEP stands for Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing, the critical systems that ensure a building's functionality and comfort.

How do BIM & MEP work together?

BIM integrates MEP designs into a coordinated 3D model, helping detect clashes, optimize layouts, and enhance project accuracy.

Who can benefit from BIM & MEP solutions?

Architects, engineers, contractors, and building owners benefit from reduced errors, faster workflows, and cost savings.

Do you provide support during construction?

Yes, we assist from design to construction, offering updated models, coordination support, and on-site guidance if needed.
